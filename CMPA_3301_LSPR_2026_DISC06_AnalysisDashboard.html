The Discussion 06 Lexicon

Our classroom surfaced powerful new ways to describe project failure and success.

The Silo of Silence

Contributed by Kordell

Where problems fester in the dark because meetings lack a safe way to surface blockers.

Dump and Hope

Contributed by Timothy

The academic failure of distributing work without tracking progress until the final deadline.

The Spotlight Shift

Contributed by Sadia & Blaire

When participants mentally check out the moment their personal update is finished.

The 1:1 Trap

Contributed by Dominique

Large group meetings hijacked by two individuals, alienating the rest of the team.

Backward Leaning

Contributed by Chris

Focusing solely on history ("What happened?") rather than proactive commitments.

The FAA Model

Contributed by Kenneth

A risk-based approach where cadence scales from monthly (design) to weekly (construction).
