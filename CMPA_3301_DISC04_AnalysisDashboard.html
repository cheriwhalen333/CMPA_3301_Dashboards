The Risk Calculation

Actual Risk = I × P

Impact (1-5)

How much does this hurt the project?

Probability (1-5)

How likely is it to happen?

Note: A score of 12 or above requires an active strategy. Scores of 1-11 are typically "monitored" or "accepted."

T.A.M.E Strategies

Transfer

Pass the risk to someone else (e.g. Insurance).

Accept

Live with it if the cost of fixing it is too high.

Mitigate

Reduce the Impact or Probability (e.g. Backups).

Eliminate

Change the project so the risk doesn't exist.
