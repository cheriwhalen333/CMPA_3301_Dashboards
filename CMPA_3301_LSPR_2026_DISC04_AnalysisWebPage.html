Risk Themes & TAME Application

This section analyzes how the cohort chose to handle the identified risks using the TAME framework. While "Mitigation" remains the standard approach, we saw several students move toward "Elimination" through technical automation and "Acceptance" for unavoidable real-life schedule conflicts.

PMUPM Chapter 4 Reference

The TAME Framework

Risk Calculation Formula

Actual Risk = Impact × Probability

Transfer

Shifting the responsibility or burden of a risk to a third party or backup system.

Accept

Acknowledging the risk exists and preparing to deal with the consequences if they occur.

Mitigate

Proactively taking steps to reduce the likelihood (probability) or the severity (impact).

Eliminate

Changing the project plan or technical approach to remove the risk entirely.
