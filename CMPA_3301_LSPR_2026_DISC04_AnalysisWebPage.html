Risk Themes & TAME Application

This section breaks down how we chose to handle our risks using the TAME method. Most of you chose to "Mitigate" (reduce) the risk, but it was great to see some students "Accepting" things they couldn't control.

Textbook Reference: PMUPM Ch. 4

How we TAME a Risk

Our Formula

Risk Score = Impact × Probability

Transfer

Passing the problem to someone else or a backup system.

Accept

Knowing the trouble might come and being ready for it.

Mitigate

Taking steps to make the risk less likely or less harmful.

Eliminate

Changing the plan to remove the risk completely.
